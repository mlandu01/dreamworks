About

Hiccup is a Viking who must kill a dragon to mark his passage into manhood and be initiated into the family tribe. However, he ends up befriending a deadly dragon called Night Fury.

Character Profiles

Hiccup

Hiccup is a young happy-go-lucky viking who goes against tradition by befriending Night Fury.

Toothless

Toothless is a rare night fury dragon. He becomes friends with Hiccup.

Astrid Hofferson

Astrid Hofferson is a tough girl with bags of energy and passion. She is determined to be the best viking she can be. She later finds out Hiccup's secret friendship with Night Fury.

Stoick the Vast

He is the father of Hiccup and the leader of the vikings in Berk.

Gobber the Belch

He is the blacksmith in Berk and a close friend of Stoick's. He is also the teacher of the tribe's young dragon-training recruits.

Fishlegs Ingerman

He is an enthusiastic boy who is very knowledgeable about dragon history.

Snotlout Jorgenson

He is one of Hiccup's dragon-training classmates. Snotlout is arrogant and sometimes rude. But he can also be reliable.

Tuffnut and Ruffnut Thorston

They are twins; a boy and a girl. They tend to argue a lot.
