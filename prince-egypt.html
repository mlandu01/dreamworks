About

Based on the Biblical story located in the book of Exodus, this film tells the story of two brothers named Moses and Rameses grow up to be the best of friends. However, when one of them becomes a ruler and the other chooses to live for the people, their friendship turns bitter.

Character Profiles

Moses

Moses is the Prince of Egypt. Adopted as a son by Pharaoh, he later learns that he has Hebrew routes and he is chosen by God to rescue the Hebrews from the very people who onced called family.

Rameses

He is the brother of Moses. They grow up together as very close brothers but when Moses decides to rescue his people, they begin to go apart.

Pharaoh Seti

He is the father of Moses and Rameses. He and Queen Tuya adopted Moses as a son.

Queen Tuya

She is the mother of Moses and Rameses. She and Pharaoh Seti adopted Moses as a son.

Miriam

Miriam is the biological sister of Moses. After seperating from birth due to the cruel rules of the previous pharaoh, the siblings reunite and this opens the eyes of Moses. He realises he is a Hebrew.

Aaron

Aaron is the biological brother of Moses. Together with Miriam, he reminds Moses of who he truly is.

Tzipporah

She is the wife of Moses. Her support and wisdom encourages Moses to do what he is destined to do.

Jethro

Jethro is the father of Tzipporah and the father-in-law to Moses. He is a very cheerful and caring man who provides a great environment for Moses during his time away from Egypt.

Hotep & Huy

They are the right-hand men to Pharaoh and provide advice and withcraft as an aid.
