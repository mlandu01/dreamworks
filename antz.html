Character Profiles

Z

A funny happy-go-lucky ant who is curious about the outside world.

Weaver

Weaver is the best friend of Z. He always has his back.

General Mandible

He is the general of the colony. He appears to be helping the ants but he has an ulterior motive.

Princess Bala

An independant ant who dares to dream as she is introduced to new possibilities by Z.

Barbatus

He is a brave veteran of the ant army. He takes Z under his wing during a campaign.

Azteca

Azteca teams up with Z and Weaver to bring down the horrible regime formed by General Mandible.

Colonel Cutter

He is the right hand ant for General Mandible. He is a stickler for rules and regulations.
