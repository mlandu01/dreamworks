About

Roddy, a rat, lives a luxurious lifestyle in a furnished flat. However, life has other plans for him after he gets flushed from a toilet into the sewers of London, where a new beginning awaits him.

Character Profiles

Roddy

Roddy is a friendly charming young rat. He has a wonderful life...until he gets flushed away into the sewers.

Rita Malone

Rita Malone is a street-wise rat who Roddy meets in the sewers. She is the captain of The Jammy Dodger.

Toad

Toad is the antagonist and has an evil plan of destruction. He wants to get rid of the population of Ratropolis so he can make room for his hundreds of off-spring.

Le Frog

He is the cousin of Toad and acompanies him everywhere.

Spike

He is one of the Toad's two top hench-rats and Whitney's partner in crime. He can be rather aggressive but also quick witted.

Whitey

He is another one of Toad's two top hence-rats and Spike's partner in crime. Unlike Spike, Whitney is less prone to anger and is more sympathetic.

Sid

Sid is a lazy and selfish rat. He is the one who flushes Roddy down the toilet.
